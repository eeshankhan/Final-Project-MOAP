Cancellation Rules & Refund Policy: 2019-20

(As per the UGC Notification on Fees Refund on Fees dated: 10-10-2018)
S.No. Percentage of Refund of Aggregate fees* Deduction as Processing Charges applicable on annual fee (Irrespective of deposited amount) Point of time when notice of withdrawal of admission is served
1 95% 5% (Subject to maximum of Rs. 5000/-) Up to July 10th, 2019
2 90% 10% July 11th to July 25th, 2019
3 80% 20% July 26th to August 09th, 2019
4 50% 50% August 10th to August 23rd, 2019
5 0% 100% August 24th, 2019 Onwards

  • * Inclusive of annual tuition fee and non-tuition fee but exclusive of caution money. Deductions were made from deposited amount according to above rules as applicable on annual fees.
  • No fees will be refunded if the application for cancellation is received after the above-mentioned date.
  • If the admitted student wishes to cancel his/her admission after the September 10th, 2019 then he/she must pay the fees for the full tenure of the program.
  • The student should maintain at least 75% attendance in the class or else he/she will not be allowed to appear in the examinations.
  • Enrolment, examination and other fees are payable separately.
  • University Blazer is compulsory from the first day of the academic session.
  • In addition to the above fees, if any taxes (GST & other taxes etc.) have been introduced by Govt; there will be charged extra to the students.
  • Refund of fees will take 15 office working days from the receipt of completed cancellation form with supportive documents.
