Rules:
  • Early bird admissions are limited and provisional in nature*
  • Merit Cutoff Relaxation would be 10% of the first round cutoff (Either in Rank/Marks/Percentage as decided by the University) for earlybird candidates.
  • Admission application fee of Rs. 1500/- is not refundable in any case.
  • If candidate is not satisfied with the program/branch he/she has been offered during allotment and he/she wishes to reappear for the next round then Rs. 500/- will be applicable as processing/counselling charge. For not alloted candidate no charge will be taken.
  • If the university is not able to allot any program/branch to the applied candidates and he/she wishes to appear for next round, then No forwarding/counselling charge would be taken.
  • Candidate can change his/her choice of programs/branch/course or any other data till the last date of online form collection for respective round with an Application Form Updation Fee of Rs. 100/- through online/offline mode (whatever is available).
  • Provisional admission through various rounds will be made by paying the Ist installment of the annual fee
  • Candidate needs to fulfill the minimum elligibility criteria given by the state/central government norms.
  • Candidate needs to follow the recent selection criterias declared/updated by the university (Round wise).
  • If the candidate fails to qualify any of the above rules/criterias or the given information is found incorrect/forged then the university retains the rights to cancel the admission.
  • For cancellation policies and deduction ratio read "Cancellation Rules & Refund Policy: 2019-20".
